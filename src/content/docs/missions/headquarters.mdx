<p class="lead">The Diocese of Kalookan Curia officials, together with dedicated mission partners, officially inaugurated the St. Therese Mission Headquarters in Malabon City.</p>

## A Sanctuary for Missionaries

The new structure was formally blessed by **Kalookan Bishop Pablo Virgilio David**. Designed to serve as a central hub for the diocese's outreach efforts, the headquarters includes:

* **Function Hall** for community gatherings.
* **Residential Bedrooms** for visiting missionaries.
* **Administrative Spaces** for coordination.

> "This headquarters is more than just a building; it is a testament to our commitment to the diocese's mission and a home for those who serve them."

## Strategic Impact

Located in Malabon, the headquarters will act as the operational heart for mission activities in the sector. It provides a permanent base for mission partners to collaborate on poverty alleviation and youth ministry.
